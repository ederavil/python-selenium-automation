How to Cook Spinach Alfredo Lasagna Roll-ups

Ingredients:

  • 8 lasagna noodle sheets
  • 4 cups baby spinach
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 5 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon avocado oil
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2/4 cup of fresh-grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon fresh-cracked black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh minced oregano
  • 1 teaspoon fresh minced thyme
  • 2 eggs lightly beaten
  • Parsley for garnish
  • 3 tablespoons of button
  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream

Steps of Instruction:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. In a large pot of salted, boiling water, cook lasagna noodles for 10 minutes .
  3. Drain, and lay flat on a sheet of wax paper until ready to fill
  4. In a large skillet, add avocado oil and onion, and cook for 3 minutes.
  5. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Add baby spinach and cook for 3 minutes, until wilted.
  7. Remove and set aside on cutting board to cool, and wipe skillet clean.
  8. Once mixture is cool, chop well
  9. In a large bowl, combine ricotta, Parmesan, spinach mixture, eggs, salt, pepper, onion powder, oregano, and thyme. Mix well
  10. In same skillet used for spinach, melt butter. Add garlic, and cook over medium-low heat for 2 minutes. Increase heat to medium, add heavy whipping cream, and cook for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. If cream starts to bubble too much, turn heat down slightly.
  11. Stir in Parmesan, and cook for 2 minutes, until sauce thickens. Turn heat to low
  12. Spoon filling mixture evenly between lasagna noodles. Be careful to leave a little bit of space around all the edges, so it doesn't spill out the sides as it bakes.
  13. Beginning at bottom end of each lasagna noodle, roll noodle all the way until fully closed.
  14. Spray a baking dish with cooking spray. Lay roll ups seam-side down in baking dish. Pour Alfredo sauce over roll ups
  15. Bake for 40 minutes. Remove from oven, and garnish with parsley. If desired, pour a little more Alfredo sauce on top. Serve immediately
